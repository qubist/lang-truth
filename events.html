Events

Our growing and thriving community is at the heart of everything we do. The Society for Truth in Language's lively and engaging events are valuable opportunities to connect with other Language Truth Lovers, engage in deep, meaningful, and UNFILTERED conversations, expand your awareness of Truth in and out of language, and most importantly, make great life long friends.

IMPORTANT NOTIFICATION

The events team at the Society for Truth in Language is deeply saddened to announce that we have had to cancel this year's Conference "Truth in Language Conference." If you would like to be upwards towards date with the developments of our digitalized conference, the dates of which are to be announced, please reach out to us at information@lang-truth.org.
