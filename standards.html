Standards

Has a word, phrase, or other linguistic item been subject to the any of the following abuses?

Inaccuracy Shortening Reduplication
Obfuscation Portmanteuing Ambiguity
Abbreviation Cutening Acronymization

If so, it is a candidate to be bathed in the linguistically healing waters of Language Truth.

Spiritual Guidance for Seekers of Language Truth:

  • Here are some axioms to guide you in your language truth journey:
  • Conjecture is better than abbreviation
  • Perfection takes as much time as it needs
  • If it's worth saying, it's worth saying as long as it takes to say it
  • Follow your gastrointestinal tract when you feel like something's not right with a word
  • Never give up. Never surrender.
