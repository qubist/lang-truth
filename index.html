Welcome

Welcome, friends, to the Society for Truth in Language's official web presence.

We are a diverse community of caring etymologists and persnickety editors who find great value in seeking the truth that lurks beneath the language we use to make and share our reality.

We live in a world of lies. They surround everyday. We cannot communicate to each in integrity unless the medium for our communication becomes integrated and saturated with the truth itself.
